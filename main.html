Introduction

”A small team of A+ players can run circles around a giant team of B and C players.”

— Steve Jobs

Talented people are the foundation of every successful company and startup in history.

The best companies view people as a core, ongoing strategy and foster a culture of hiring as a way to complement skills and catalyse behaviours.

These are the companies that reach the tallest peaks.

The companies that fail don’t do so because of a lack of ambition or vision, but as the result of poor execution.

It’s like embarking on a journey without a map.

In practice, having a map means having a predictable, scalable system in place for every step of the recruiting and hiring journey - from sourcing & interviewing, compensation & benefits to onboarding & retention.

There’s no one-size-fits-all approach to recruiting and each company develops their own process to align with their unique culture.

Having a clearly defined hiring process can help early startups avoid costly and destabilising hiring mistakes.

This guide will provide you with the roadmap to successful hiring and support your every step of your recruiting journey.

Whether it’s sourcing only the right people or convincing a high-quality candidate to join your early-stage team, you’ll know the right move. Here you have a high-level breakdown of an end-to-end recruiting process for early hires.
—
It might not be easy, but with careful attention, you can make sure your first hires help you find Product-Market Fit and shape a great culture in your company from the start, for years to come.

This Field Guide, together with the documentation enclosed, is exactly what you need to start recruiting your BEST FOUNDING TEAM.
